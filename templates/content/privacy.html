This page describes the stored user information, which is further processed by the "Text To Morse" website.

Information storage

We do not store users personal data on our servers. All necessary information is processed during initial authorization and stored in a secure cookie on the client's side.

Use of information

All data is used exclusively for the operation of the Website and its functionality.

Storage and Use of Cookies

We use cookies to ensure the operation of the website and to protect it by the Cloudflare service.

Cloudflare provides CDN services, secure access to resources, and protection against DDoS attacks. They can set their own cookies, which are used to identify customers by a common IP address and apply some security settings for each customer.

Deleting Cookies

You can clear the saved cookies in your browser settings (use your browser's help). Keep in mind that disabling cookies will affect the functionality of this and many other websites you visit.

Feedback

If you do not agree with the processing of your data, we recommend that you refrain from using the Site.

Policy Changes

We may update the Policy from time to time without further notice to users. We recommend checking this page regularly for changes.
